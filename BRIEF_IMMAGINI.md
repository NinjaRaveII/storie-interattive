# Brief immagini — L'Oasi delle Sabbie Dorate & La Campana d'Oro del Villaggio

> Prompt pronti da incollare in un generatore di immagini a tua scelta (ChatGPT immagini, Bing Image Creator, Midjourney, ecc.), seguendo le regole di `GUIDA_STORIE.md` §6.
> **Formato:** panoramico largo ~1600×500 (o 1920×600), soggetto centrato con margini di sicurezza ai bordi, niente testo nell'immagine, `.jpg` ottimizzato.
> **Coerenza personaggio:** genera le 5 immagini di ogni storia nella stessa sessione, partendo se possibile da un ritratto di riferimento del protagonista (vedi metodo in `GUIDA_STORIE.md` §6). Prima di salvare, apri le 5 immagini affiancate e verifica che il protagonista sia identico ovunque (specie/aspetto, colori, vestiti).

## Preambolo di stile (da anteporre a ogni prompt)

```
STYLE: dreamy dark-fantasy storybook illustration, warm muted palette,
soft golden light, gentle and child-friendly, painterly, cozy and magical,
soft depth of field, no text, wide cinematic 16:5 composition.
```

## Storia 1 — L'Oasi delle Sabbie Dorate (`images/oasis/`)

### Scheda personaggio — Sara (protagonista, presente in tutte e 5 le immagini)

```
Sara: a young girl around 8 years old, warm light-brown skin, dark curly hair
tied back with a red patterned scarf, wearing a sand-colored linen tunic with
a small turquoise satchel backpack, simple leather sandals, warm brown eyes,
gentle and determined expression.
```

### Schede personaggi secondari (una sola scena ciascuno)

```
Sabbiosa (volpe di sabbia): a small fox made of warm golden desert sand,
amber-orange ear tips, glowing amber eyes, faint sparkle trailing off her fur
like fine sand dust.

Yusuf (vecchio cammelliere) e Vento (il suo cammello): an elderly man with a
deeply lined kind face, short white beard, flowing indigo-blue desert robe,
cream turban, seated beside his calm brown camel with a woven red saddle
blanket.

Zefira (spirito del vento): a bird made of shimmering golden light and
swirling sand-dust, translucent glowing wings, no visible feet, trailing
sparkles like a comet.
```

### 1. `intro.jpg`
```
[STYLE] [Sara]. Sara stands at the edge of a dried-up desert oasis at dawn,
an empty clay jug in her hands, looking down at a small silver fish resting
on the cracked sand, golden dunes stretching into the distance, a sense of
gentle worry and wonder.
```

### 2. `middle_dune.jpg`
```
[STYLE] [Sara] [Sabbiosa]. Sara kneels beside an ancient stone archway covered
in glowing carvings, next to Sabbiosa the golden sand fox, warm late-afternoon
desert light, dunes framing the scene.
```

### 3. `middle_camel.jpg`
```
[STYLE] [Sara] [Yusuf e Vento]. Sara sits cross-legged in the shade of a
striped desert tent, listening to old Yusuf tell a story, his camel Vento
resting peacefully nearby, warm firelight glow, cozy desert evening
atmosphere.
```

### 4. `middle_wind.jpg`
```
[STYLE] [Sara] [Zefira]. Sara rides on the back of Zefira, a shimmering bird
made of golden light and sand-dust, soaring above rolling desert dunes at
sunset, trails of golden sparkles in the sky.
```

### 5. `end.jpg` (condivisa dai 9 finali — scena neutra e trionfale)
```
[STYLE] [Sara]. Sara stands smiling at the edge of a now-flourishing desert
oasis, clear blue water reflecting golden light, palm trees and blooming
flowers around the spring, a sense of triumphant warmth and renewal.
```

## Storia 2 — La Campana d'Oro del Villaggio (`images/bell/`)

### Scheda personaggio — Tobia (protagonista, presente in tutte e 5 le immagini)

```
Tobia: a boy around 8 years old, light skin with rosy cheeks, short curly
chestnut-brown hair, wearing a mustard-yellow tunic with a brown leather
vest, red neckerchief, brown trousers, simple leather boots, bright curious
green eyes.
```

### Schede personaggi secondari (una sola scena ciascuno)

```
Mastro Nando (guardiano della torre): an elderly bell-tower keeper, stooped
posture, round spectacles, deep burgundy overcoat with brass buttons, holding
a small lantern, kind wrinkled face.

Allodola dorata (Piuma): a small lark with feathers of soft shimmering gold,
a tiny tuft on its head, bright black eyes, delicate and shy posture.
```

### 1. `intro.jpg`
```
[STYLE] [Tobia]. Tobia stands in the village square at dawn, looking up at a
golden castle bell tower in the distance, wilted flowers in the market stalls
around him, a hush over the usually cheerful village, warm early morning
light.
```

### 2. `middle_tower.jpg`
```
[STYLE] [Tobia] [Mastro Nando]. Tobia and old Mastro Nando kneel together
inside the cozy bell tower chamber, cradling a small cracked golden egg
nestled in straw, warm lantern light, old gears and bell ropes in the
background.
```

### 3. `middle_florist.jpg`
```
[STYLE] [Tobia]. Tobia holds a softly glowing golden flower given to him by
the market florist, standing among flower stalls and baskets in the golden-
castle village square, warm afternoon light, gentle curiosity on his face.
```

### 4. `middle_feathers.jpg`
```
[STYLE] [Tobia] [Piuma]. Tobia crouches gently in a blooming rose-garden maze
inside the castle grounds, discovering the small shy golden lark hiding among
the petals, soft dappled sunlight, warm and tender atmosphere.
```

### 5. `end.jpg` (condivisa dai 9 finali — scena neutra e trionfale)
```
[STYLE] [Tobia]. Tobia stands joyfully in the village square as the golden
bell rings atop the castle tower, villagers and flowers blooming all around,
warm golden light pouring over the golden castle, a sense of joyful
celebration.
```
